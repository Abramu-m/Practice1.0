🗂️ Clinical Decision Support
1. Medication-related Safety

Drug–drug interaction checker (basic rules DB / external API)

Drug–allergy checker (match prescribed meds vs allergy list)

Duplicate therapy alerts (same ATC class prescribed twice)

Dose range checker (pediatric/renal/hepatic dosing tables)

Formulary guidance (suggest in-stock alternatives)

Predict adverse drug reactions (AI trained on patient history)

Medication adherence prediction

2. Evidence-based Guidelines & Reminders

Order sets/templates (predefined meds + labs for common conditions)

Basic guideline prompts (hypertension, diabetes, immunizations)

Preventive care reminders (screenings, vaccinations, check-ups)

Condition-based dashboards (e.g., diabetes → HbA1c trends, BP control)

Personalized guideline recommendations (AI considers comorbidities)

Auto-adaptation to local/national guidelines

3. Diagnostics & Risk Stratification

Built-in calculators (BMI, eGFR, CHA₂DS₂-VASc, Wells score, etc.)

Lab result highlighting (out-of-range, critical values)

Diagnostic support: suggest differential diagnoses from symptoms/labs

Imaging guidance prompts (CT/MRI appropriateness checks)

AI-driven diagnosis support (NLP on notes, lab trends, imaging analysis)

Early-warning systems (e.g., sepsis detection using vitals + labs)

4. Safety & Quality Monitoring

Tiered alerts (critical vs info) → reduce alert fatigue

Audit trail of clinician responses to alerts

Clinical quality measure tracking (HbA1c in diabetics, BP control rates)

Safety dashboards (per ward, per doctor, per patient group)

Predictive safety models (e.g., deterioration prediction)

Machine learning for custom risk models

5. Chronic Disease Management

Condition dashboards (diabetes, HTN, heart failure) with trends

Medication adherence reminders (SMS, app notifications)

Integrated care pathways (follow-up scheduling, labs)

Population-level monitoring (cohort reports, control rates)

AI predicting disease progression (e.g., CKD stage progression)

Personalized treatment optimization (suggesting med adjustments)

6. Patient-Centered Tools

Patient education materials (auto-generate based on diagnosis)

Simple lifestyle recommendations (diet, exercise, smoking cessation)

Multilingual patient instructions (localized handouts)

Patient portal integration (reminders, education, progress tracking)

AI chatbot for patient self-care guidance

Predict adherence risk & suggest interventions

7. Workflow & Operational Support

Clinical pathway guidance (chest pain → ECG + troponin + rule-out ACS)

Discharge checklist generator (follow-up, meds, education)

Referral prompts (specialist indications, automatic referral notes)

Care coordination reminders (nurse tasks, social worker involvement)

Adaptive clinical pathways (AI updates steps based on outcomes)

Integration with external health networks (referrals, transfers)
